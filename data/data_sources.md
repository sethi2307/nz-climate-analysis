# Data Sources

## 1. Stats NZ - Rainfall Indicator ⭐ PRIMARY
- URL: https://www.stats.govt.nz/indicators/rainfall/
- Coverage: 30 sites, 1960-2022
- Data includes:
  - Annual and seasonal rainfall
  - Daily rainfall
  - Trends and anomalies
- Status: Ready to download

## 2. Stats NZ - Temperature Indicator ⭐ PRIMARY
- URL: https://www.stats.govt.nz/indicators/temperature/
- Coverage: 
  - National series: 1909-2022 (7-station)
  - 30 sites: 1972-2022
- Data includes:
  - Daily, annual, seasonal temperatures
  - Min/max temperatures
  - Warm days (>25°C)
  - Trends and anomalies
- Status: Ready to download

## 3. NIWA CliFlo (BACKUP)
- URL: https://cliflo.niwa.co.nz/
- Account: Registered
- Status: Use if Stats NZ data insufficient
